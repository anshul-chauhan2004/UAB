About UAB

Discover the rich history, innovative vision, and impactful contributions of the University of Alabama at Birmingham.

Our History

Founded in 1969, UAB has transformed from a small extension center into a doctoral research university and academic medical center. Our institution has been at the forefront of groundbreaking discoveries in medicine, science, and engineering for over five decades.

Our Vision

At UAB, we envision a future where knowledge, innovation, and compassion converge to solve global challenges. Our mission is to be a research university and academic health center that discovers, teaches, and applies knowledge for the intellectual, cultural, social, and economic benefit of Birmingham, the state, and beyond.

Our Impact

UAB's influence extends far beyond our campus, touching lives and driving progress in numerous fields.

Research Breakthroughs

Our researchers are at the forefront of scientific discovery, from developing new cancer treatments to pioneering advancements in neuroscience.

Community Engagement

Through outreach programs and partnerships, we're actively improving health outcomes and educational opportunities in our local communities.

Economic Development

As one of the largest employers in Alabama, UAB contributes significantly to the state's economy and fosters innovation and entrepreneurship.

Global Reach

Our international collaborations and study abroad programs are expanding UAB's influence on a global scale, preparing students for a interconnected world.
